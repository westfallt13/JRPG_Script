# These are for special items that have unique effects, such as the "Guide To Usable Items" which allows the player to use items in battle. These items will have their own unique scripts and dictionaries to handle their specific effects and interactions within the game. #


# Defensive items #
This folder will contain items that provide **defensive benefits** to the player, party members, such as defense boosts for calculations in regards to damage taken from enemies. These items will have their own **unique scripts** and **dictionaries** to handle their specific effects and interactions within the game.


# Offensive items #
This folder will contain items that provide **offensive benefits** to the player, party members, such at attack boosts, crit rate boosts for x amount of turns, etc; these items will have their own **unique scripts** and **dictionaries** to handle their specific effects and interactions within the game. 
### (Can also contain items that deal damage to enemies, such as bombs, grenades, etc; these items will also have their own **unique scripts** and **dictionaries** to handle their specific effects and interactions within the game.) ###
